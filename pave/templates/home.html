Welcome to PAVE

The Project Announcement Visibility Effort (PAVE) is designed to openly solicit applicants for high profile assignments which may lead to greater visibility and career enhancement for the PAVE applicant. The PAVE program enables NASA managers and supervisors to advertise special opportunities to NASA civil servants.
