Purpose

PAVE is designed to openly solicit applicants for high profile assignments which may lead to greater visibility and career enhancement for the PAVE applicant. The PAVE program enables NASA managers and supervisors to advertise special opportunities to NASA civil servants.

Please Note PAVE is not a part of the merit promotion and competitive placement plan
