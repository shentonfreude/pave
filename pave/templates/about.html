Purpose

PAVE is designed to openly solicit applicants for high profile assignments which may lead to greater visibility and career enhancement for the PAVE applicant. The PAVE program enables NASA managers and supervisors to advertise special opportunities to NASA civil servants.

Please Note PAVE is not a part of the merit promotion and competitive placement plan.

Who is Eligible?

The PAVE communicates to NASA Civil Servants, opportunities where interested parties can apply on-line with minimal effort. PAVE does not replace Competitive Placement Plan vacancy announcements that require competition through merit promotion procedures.

What is PAVE?

The Project Announcement Visibility Effort (PAVE) is a program that enables NASA managers and NASA supervisors to advertise special opportunities to NASA Civil Servants. These opportunities are details or one time only special projects that have an Objective, Who to Contact for questions, a Start Date, an End Date, Skill Mix and a Detailed Description. A NASA supervisor/manager can specifically target an announcement/opportunity to a specific audience(s) (i.e. Series, Grade, Pay Plan and/or all of NASA or certain Centers) through the system.

Restrictions

Employees must seek their Supervisor's approval prior to applying to a PAVE opportunity.

NASA Full-time Permanent Civil Servants only can apply.
